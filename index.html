Student Leave Tracker System

About This System

This online leave Tracker system allows students to easily apply for leave by submitting a leave application along with supporting documents. Faculty members can review and approve or reject leave requests, while HODs provide final approval. It ensures a transparent, organized, and paperless leave process within the college campus.

Quick Instructions
  • Students: Login using your Roll Number and Password to apply for leave or check your leave history.
  • Faculty: Review student leave requests and approve or reject them.
  • HOD: Provide final approval or rejection for faculty-reviewed leave requests.
  • Supported leave types include: Casual Leave, Sick Leave, Academic Leave, and more.
  • Attach required supporting documents such as medical certificates or event invitations when necessary.
